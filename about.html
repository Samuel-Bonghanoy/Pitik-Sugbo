What is Pitik Sugbo?

Pitik Sugbo is a website and platform that allows Cebuano photographers to share their best works. This platform also challenges dedicated photographers to share their best works in order for us to showcase it on our website. It may also help them learn and enhance their talents even more. Overall, Pitik Sugbo is a great opportunity for photographers to challenge themselves by participating in this monthly contest based in Cebu. With our ability to promote their work by sharing it in our online gallery, many photographers will be drawn in to contribute for this platform.
